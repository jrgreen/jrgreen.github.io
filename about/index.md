Our group is a collection of professional scientists solving problems.
This site is to give you a general sense of our current work.

### Tools we use and develop

**Simulations**
- Molecular dynamics
- Reaction-diffusion
- Stochastic chemical kinetics

> If you're interested in these tools (and chemistry, physics, data science, or applied mathematics), head over to [Contact](./contact) and drop us a line.

### Location
Our group is on the oceanfront campus of the University of Massachusetts, Boston.
We are minutes from downtown Boston.
Away from the whiteboard, much work happens online with Slack, Zoom, and Github.
